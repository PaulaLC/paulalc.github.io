what I do.

I create and design Data Science and AI products that empower businesses to make informed, data-driven decisions.

who I am.

Passionate about Data, Technology, and Innovation, I've been contributing my expertise in Tech industry since my graduation in Statistics a decade ago to renowned companies such as TikTok, Apple and Telefónica. My journey has been filled with exciting projects, including Recommender Systems and E-Commerce. I'm also pursuing a PhD in Mathematical Optimization applied to Financial Economics.

I'm on a mission to inspire and educate others in the realms of Data Visualization and Data Science. I've created the blog El arte del dato bien contado (The Art of Well-Told Data), where I share insights, tips, and best practices about dataviz. Additionally, I love engaging with the community through technical and outreach talks, spreading the knowledge and excitement about these fields.

I firmly believe that data has the potential to shape a better world.
